# Loyalty Program Benchmarks Research

## Sephora Beauty Insider (40M+ members)
- **Structure:** 3 tiers based on ANNUAL SPENDING
- **Earn rate:** 1 point per $1 spent
- **Tiers:**
  - Insider: $0 (free to join) - basic perks, birthday gift
  - VIB: $350/year - monthly gifts, early access, free shipping $35+
  - Rouge: $1,000/year - unlimited free shipping, 4x points events, $100 off for 2500 pts, exclusive events
- **Redemption:** 2500 points = $100 off (Rouge only), 500 pts = $10 reward
- **Key insight:** Tiers based on SPENDING not points. Points are for rewards, spending determines tier.

## Starbucks Rewards (2026 update)
- **Earn rate:** 1 Star per $1 spent (was 2 stars per $1)
- **Bonus:** 10 Stars for $30-49.99 reload, 25 Stars for $50+ reload
- **Redemption tiers (not membership tiers):**
  - 60 Stars: $2 off any purchase
  - 100 Stars: brewed coffee or bakery (up to $6)
  - 200 Stars: handcrafted drink or hot breakfast (up to $10)
  - 300 Stars: lunch item (up to $16)
  - 400 Stars: select merchandise or packaged coffee
- **Key insight:** Single membership level, Stars determine what you can redeem. Very low barrier.

## Vivid Seats (Ticketing)
- **Structure:** Buy 10 tickets, 11th is free (averaged price)
- **Key insight:** Simple, easy to understand. No complex points.

## Fnac+ (France, entertainment/ticketing)
- **Structure:** Paid membership (€9.99/year for Fnac+, €49/year for Fnac+ One)
- **Benefits:** 
  - 5% cashback on purchases
  - Free express delivery
  - Reduced prices on shows/concerts
  - Exclusive access to pre-sales
- **Key insight:** Paid membership model, not points-based. Immediate tangible benefits.

## Clappit (Event ticketing platform)
- **Structure:** Organizer assigns points per event/product/action
- **Points for actions:** Registration, newsletter subscription, ticket scan at entry
- **Variable points:** Can double points for slow-selling events
- **Redemption:** Drinks, food, discount vouchers for future shows
- **Key insight:** Points per EVENT not per currency. Flexible for organizers.

## Key Takeaways for TicketByLamako

1. **Spending-based tiers** (like Sephora) are more standard than points-based tiers
2. **Low entry barrier** is critical - first tier should be achievable within 1-2 purchases
3. **Tangible rewards** matter more than abstract points (free tickets, upgrades, drinks)
4. **Event-specific bonuses** (like Clappit) make sense for ticketing
5. **Simple math** - customers need to easily calculate their rewards
6. **Annual reset** keeps engagement high (Sephora requalifies yearly)

## Proposed Structure for TicketByLamako (Madagascar context)

Average ticket price in Madagascar: ~30,000 - 150,000 Ar
Average annual spend per active customer (estimated): 100,000 - 500,000 Ar

### Earn Rate
- 1 point per 1,000 Ar spent (keep current - simple math)
- Bonus: +50 pts registration, +5 pts per event attended (scan), +10 pts review

### Tier Thresholds (based on TOTAL SPENDING, not just points)
Need to be achievable:
- Fan (Bronze): 0 pts (free join) - ~0 Ar spent
- VIP (Silver): 100 pts - ~100,000 Ar spent (1-2 events)
- Super VIP (Gold): 500 pts - ~500,000 Ar spent (3-5 events)
- Elite (Platinum): 2,000 pts - ~2,000,000 Ar spent (10+ events over time)

### Redemption
- 50 pts = 5,000 Ar discount (more accessible)
- 100 pts = 12,000 Ar discount (better rate at higher volume)
- 200 pts = 30,000 Ar discount (even better rate)
- Special: 500 pts = Free standard ticket to select events
