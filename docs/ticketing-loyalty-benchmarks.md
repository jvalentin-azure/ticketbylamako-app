# Ticketing Loyalty Program Benchmarks

## Otayo (Mauritius - Direct Competitor in Island Market)

### Tiers (Points-based, NOT spend-based)
| Tier | Points Required | Key Benefit |
|------|----------------|-------------|
| Bronze | 0-1000 | Basic: earn points, redeem cash discounts, birthday bonus |
| Silver | 1001-2000 | + Special member discounts |
| Gold | 2001-5000 | + Invites to exclusive events, early access |
| Platinum | 5001-10000 | + Ticket upgrades, 25% extra points on purchases |
| Diamond | 10001+ | + 50% extra points on purchases |

### How Points are Earned
| Action | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Create account | 100 pts |
| Complete profile | 100 pts |
| Subscribe newsletter | 100 pts |
| First purchase | 200 pts (bonus) |
| Purchase tickets | "Subject to Purchase Amount" (unclear rate) |
| Share event on social media | 20 pts |
| Birthday | 200 pts |
| Coupons | Variable |

### Key Observations
- 5 tiers (more granular than most)
- Points are NOT directly tied to spend amount (it says "subject to purchase amount" but doesn't specify rate)
- Heavy emphasis on non-purchase actions (profile, newsletter, sharing)
- Birthday bonus is generous (200 pts)
- Multipliers only at top tiers (25% at Platinum, 50% at Diamond)
- No clear redemption rate published (cash discounts but amount unclear)

## Live Nation All Access (Global Leader)

### Structure
- FREE membership (no tiers based on spend)
- Earn "Concert Cash" after attending shows
- $25 off next Live Nation show after attending one
- Access to 20,000+ presales
- Simple: attend → get reward → use on next show

### Key Observations
- No complex tier system
- Direct cashback model ($25 per show attended)
- Focus on presale access as main benefit
- Very simple to understand

## Audience Rewards (Ticketmaster/Broadway)

### Structure
- Earn ShowPoints: 2 pts per $1 on ticket purchases
- VIP tier at 10,000 pts/year: 3 pts per $1, 50% more points
- Redeem for: free tickets, merch, dining, meet & greets
- Points can also be PURCHASED

### Key Observations
- Clear earn rate: 2 pts per $1
- Only ONE VIP tier (10,000 pts = $5,000 spend/year)
- Points purchasable (revenue stream for company)
- Experiential rewards (meet & greets, backstage)

## AMC Stubs (Cinema - Entertainment)

### Structure
- Insider: FREE tier, 20 pts per $1
- Premiere: $15/year, earn faster, birthday popcorn
- A-List: $24.99/month, 4 movies/week, no fees

### Key Observations
- Paid tiers generate direct revenue
- 5,000 pts = $5 reward (so $250 spend = $5 back = 2% return)
- Very low return rate for company benefit

## ANALYSIS FOR TICKETBYLAMAKO

### What works for ticketing companies:
1. **Low return rate** - 1-2% cashback equivalent maximum
2. **High thresholds** - Make tiers aspirational but achievable over 1-2 years
3. **Non-monetary rewards** - Early access, upgrades cost nothing but feel premium
4. **Simple earn rate** - 1 pt per X Ar spent (easy to understand)
5. **Experiential rewards** - Backstage, meet & greet (zero cost to company)

### Recommended for LamakoRewards (Madagascar context):
- Average ticket price: 30,000-150,000 Ar
- Average customer buys: 2-4 events/year
- Annual spend per customer: 60,000-600,000 Ar

### Proposed Conservative Tiers:
| Tier | Threshold | Typical Customer |
|------|-----------|-----------------|
| Fan | 0 pts | Just signed up |
| Silver | 500 pts | 1-2 events (500,000 Ar spend) |
| Gold | 2,000 pts | 5-8 events over time (2,000,000 Ar) |
| Platinum | 5,000 pts | 15+ events, loyal customer (5,000,000 Ar) |
| Diamond | 10,000 pts | Top 1% superfan |

### Earn Rate: 1 pt per 1,000 Ar (same as Otayo model)
- 30,000 Ar ticket = 30 pts
- 150,000 Ar VIP ticket = 150 pts

### Redemption: Conservative
- 500 pts = 10,000 Ar discount (20 Ar/pt = 2% return)
- This means spending 500,000 Ar gets you 10,000 Ar back = 2% cashback equivalent

### Multipliers (only at high tiers):
- Fan/Silver: x1
- Gold: x1.25 (25% bonus)
- Platinum: x1.5 (50% bonus)  
- Diamond: x2 (100% bonus)
